Abstract

Early detection of liver tumor is very important in order to save the thousands of lives. For that we should find out the technique which will provide high accuracy in order to earlier recognition of liver tumor. There were more than 9,00, 000 new cases of liver cancer in 2020. Earlier recognition of liver tumor will grow life expectations. We studied different techniques of liver tumor detection, we compared all the methods and the accuracies achieved by them. Also we compared the accuracies achieved by using different datasets. We have made comparative study of the methods like modified u-net, ResU-Net, SegNet, Hybridized Fully Convolutional Neural Network(HFCNN), deep neural network(DNN), etc.

Introduction

One of the major reason of death in the globe is liver tumor. It takes time an is crucial to diagnose liver cancers in their early stages. In order to get an accurate and effective diagnosis, it is important to have a mechanized approach for the detection of liver cancers.As the tumor cells flaccid by the blood are highly reachable to the liver. Liver cancer in its early stages starts growing in the liver but ultimately begin expanding to the whole body parts. This is the basic origin of liver tumors, So it is very important to determine the liver tumor.

Literature Survey

We studied different research papers regarding liver tumor detection. We found many deep learning techniques for detection of tumor. We studied the accuracies generating on different datasets.we observed that ResU-net shows high accuracy as compared to other models. ResU-Net shows 99.8% accuracy using 3D-IRCADb01 dataset.
